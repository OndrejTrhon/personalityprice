Have you ever thought about how much does your data cost?
Please enter your value in whole dollars. We will then ask you to login to Facebook to count your price. Switch off blocking popups.
How does it work?

1. Estimation

We will ask you to estimate your personal worth. Then, we will ask you to login to our website using Facebook.

2. Calculation

Through Facebook, we read the data stored on your profile - just reading, no saving. We assing each piece of data a price according to our methodology (see About). The only data we save is your total price and total estimation, anonymously.

3. Results

We print the total price (sum of prices of data pieces) together with your estimation and averages for all users of our app. The averages are the reason we save your prices. But, again, we cannot identify you in any way.
This project is a part of Big Bang Data exhibition in DOX gallery in Prague.
